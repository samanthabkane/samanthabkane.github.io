Samantha B. Kane, PhD

Postdoctoral Fellow, Annenberg Institute, University

I am a postdoctoral fellow whose work is in the economics of education and labor economics. My research asks how public policies (e.g., education, health, environmental) affect outcomes in K-12 and higher education and the workforce.

At Annenberg, I am developing research at the intersection of education and climate change. Before this position, I completed my PhD at Harvard University, where my dissertation examined the effect of reproductive rights laws on women's college choices.
